Synopsis

"The School of the Open" explores the transformative power of nature and community. Set against the backdrop of an alternative education program, the film follows a group of students as they navigate the challenges of adolescence, identity, and the search for meaning in a rapidly changing world.

Through intimate cinematography and raw, honest storytelling, the film invites viewers to question what it truly means to learn, to grow, and to be open to the unexpected lessons life has to offer.
