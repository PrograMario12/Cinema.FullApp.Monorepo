About the Film

The Story

"The School of the Open" delves into the heart of an experimental education system that prioritizes nature, self-governance, and emotional intelligence over standardized testing. Filmed over the course of a year, the documentary captures the highs and lows of a community striving to redefine success.

The film challenges conventional wisdom about education and upbringing, asking the audience to reflect on their own experiences and the systems that shape us.

Director's Statement

"I wanted to make a film that felt like a memory—something hazy yet visceral. 'The School of the Open' isn't just about a school; it's about the feeling of being young and the infinite possibilities that lie in the open spaces of our lives." — Alex Rivera, Director
